# Stems, Lyrics, and Timeline

Short answer: feedpak files can include more than notes. They may also include audio stems, lyrics, timing maps, sections, tempo, and related song data.

## Stems

Stems are separate audio tracks such as:

- Full mix.
- Vocals.
- Drums.
- Bass.
- Guitar.
- Other.

Not every song includes stems.

## Lyrics

Lyrics may include timed words, lines, translations, or transliterations depending on the song package and format version.

## Timeline

Timeline data may include:

- Beats.
- Sections.
- Tempo changes.
- Time signature changes.
- Keys or harmony where available.

## Why This Data Matters

Plugins use this data for lyrics, stem mixing, section navigation, metronome behavior, and practice views.
