## What To Ask For

When helping a user, ask for:

- FeedBack build.
- Platform.
- Input/output device.
- Song and arrangement if relevant.
- What they expected.
- What happened.
- Steps to reproduce.
- Screenshot or video if visual.
- Diagnostics bundle.

## Diagnostics

Users should reproduce the issue first, then export diagnostics from the same session.

See [Export Diagnostics](docs/first-run/export-diagnostics.md).
